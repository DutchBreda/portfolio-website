Hi, my name is Johannes, but my friends call me Dutch (or Hans). I am from The Netherlands and since September 2019 I also became an American citizen. I've been on the Internet since 1993 and tried to follow all the latest developments which made me kinda all-round. In the late 90-ies I was the founder and administrator of a international IRC (Internet Relay Chat) with servers in the Netherlands, UK, Australia and USA. Unfortunately I had to quit in 2001 because of the growing popularity of Instant Messengers and later the rise of social media like Facebook. From that period I got interested in creating websites...I started with CMS systems like Drupal, Mambo (later Joomla) and the last few years I'm using Wordpress. I seldom had to look into the code and was relying on developers of themes and plugins to help me out when there were certain issues. Now I want to be capable of fixing problems myself and enhancing the websites myself and gather the knowledge to become a Full Stack Web Developer, and find a dream job!
